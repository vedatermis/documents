# Privacy Policy for Ezan Vakti Pro

**Last Updated:** November 10, 2025

## Introduction

Ezan Vakti Pro ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Purpose:** To calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic location
- **Type:** Approximate location (city-level precision)
- **Storage:** Location data is processed locally on your device and is NOT sent to any server
- **Frequency:** Only when you use the app and grant location permission

### Notification Data
- **Purpose:** To send prayer time reminders and adhan notifications
- **Type:** Local notification scheduling based on prayer times
- **Storage:** Notification preferences are stored locally on your device

### Cache Data
- **Prayer Times:** Cached locally on your device to improve performance
- **Quran Text & Audio:** Downloaded surahs are stored locally for offline access
- **User Preferences:** Settings and preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
All data is stored **exclusively on your device**. We do NOT:
- Collect personal information
- Store data on remote servers
- Share data with third parties
- Track user behavior
- Use analytics services
- Display advertisements

### Third-Party Services
The app uses the following Apple services:
- **Location Services:** For determining prayer times (iOS system service)
- **User Notifications:** For prayer time alerts (iOS system service)
- **StoreKit:** For app rating functionality (iOS system service)

The app fetches prayer times from:
- **Aladhan API** (https://aladhan.com) - Prayer times calculation
- **Quran API** - Quranic text and translations

These services may have their own privacy policies. We do not control or have responsibility for their practices.

## Permissions Required

### Location Permission
- **Why:** To calculate accurate prayer times for your location
- **When:** Only when you use features that require location (prayer times, qibla compass)
- **Control:** You can revoke this permission anytime in iOS Settings

### Notification Permission
- **Why:** To alert you for prayer times with adhan sounds
- **When:** Only when you enable prayer notifications
- **Control:** You can disable notifications anytime in app Settings or iOS Settings

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Data Deletion
- All data is stored locally on your device
- Deleting the app removes all associated data
- You can clear cache manually in Settings > Data & Cache

### Location Services
- Disable in: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Ezan Vakti Pro

### Notifications
- Disable in: iOS Settings > Notifications > Ezan Vakti Pro
- Or in-app: Settings > Notifications

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains Islamic religious content suitable for all ages.

## Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Data persists as long as the app is installed
- Deleting the app removes all data permanently
- No data is retained on external servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Posting the new policy in the app
- Notifying through app updates

## International Users

The app is available worldwide and complies with:
- GDPR (European Union)
- CCPA (California)
- KVKK (Turkey)
- General privacy best practices

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

**Email:** vedatermis@gmail.com

## Your Consent

By using Ezan Vakti Pro, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

# Gizlilik Politikası - Ezan Vakti Pro

## Topladığımız Bilgiler

### Konum Bilgisi
- **Amaç:** Coğrafi konumunuza göre doğru namaz vakitlerini hesaplamak
- **Tür:** Yaklaşık konum (şehir düzeyinde hassasiyet)
- **Depolama:** Konum verisi cihazınızda yerel olarak işlenir ve hiçbir sunucuya GÖNDERİLMEZ
- **Sıklık:** Yalnızca uygulamayı kullandığınızda ve konum iznini verdiğinizde

### Bildirim Verileri
- **Amaç:** Namaz vakti hatırlatmaları ve ezan bildirimleri göndermek
- **Tür:** Namaz vakitlerine göre yerel bildirim planlaması
- **Depolama:** Bildirim tercihleri cihazınızda yerel olarak saklanır

### Önbellek Verileri
- **Namaz Vakitleri:** Performansı artırmak için cihazınızda önbelleğe alınır
- **Kur'an Metni ve Sesi:** İndirilen sureler çevrimdışı erişim için yerel olarak saklanır
- **Kullanıcı Tercihleri:** Ayarlar ve tercihler iOS UserDefaults kullanılarak yerel olarak saklanır

## Veri Depolama ve Güvenlik

### Yalnızca Yerel Depolama
Tüm veriler **yalnızca cihazınızda** saklanır. BİZ:
- Kişisel bilgi toplamayız
- Verileri uzak sunucularda saklamayız
- Verileri üçüncü taraflarla paylaşmayız

- Reklam göstermeyiz

### Üçüncü Taraf Servisleri
Uygulama aşağıdaki Apple servislerini kullanır:
- **Konum Servisleri:** Namaz vakitlerini belirlemek için (iOS sistem servisi)
- **Kullanıcı Bildirimleri:** Namaz vakti uyarıları için (iOS sistem servisi)
- **StoreKit:** Uygulama değerlendirme işlevi için (iOS sistem servisi)

Uygulama namaz vakitlerini şu kaynaklardan alır:
- **Aladhan API** (https://aladhan.com) - Namaz vakitleri hesaplama
