# Privacy Policy for ActMon

**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

ActMon is a macOS menu bar system monitor developed by Vedat Ermis. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ActMon handles your data.

## Data Collection

**ActMon does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** All system information displayed by the app is processed locally on your device and never leaves your Mac.

## What ActMon Accesses

ActMon accesses the following system information solely for display purposes within the app:

- CPU usage and processor information
- Memory (RAM) usage statistics
- Network upload/download speeds
- Battery status and charge level
- GPU information
- Thermal and fan status
- Disk usage
- Bluetooth device names
- Running processes and their resource usage

All of this data is read in real time, displayed to you, and **never recorded, stored, or transmitted** to any server or third party.

## Data Export

ActMon includes an optional export feature that allows you to save system information as JSON or CSV files. This export is initiated only by you and saved locally to a location you choose. No data is sent anywhere automatically.

## Network Usage

ActMon makes a single, periodic HTTP HEAD request to Apple's captive portal URL (`https://www.apple.com/library/test/success.html`) solely to measure network latency (ping). No personal data is included in this request.

## Third-Party Services

ActMon does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

## User Preferences

App settings and preferences (such as accent color, alert thresholds, and display options) are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data is not accessible to anyone other than you.

## Children's Privacy

ActMon does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
