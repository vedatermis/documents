# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: August 14, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the NobetciEczanem mobile application ("App") collects, uses, stores, and protects users' personal data. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

## 2. Data Controller

**Data Controller:** Vedat Ermis  
**Contact:** vedatermis@hotmail.com  
**Application:** NobetciEczanem

## 3. Data Collected

### 3.1 Location Data
- **Purpose:** To show you the nearest on-duty pharmacies
- **Collected Data:** GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
- **Permission:** Location permission is requested while using the app
- **Storage:** Location data is temporarily stored on your device and is not permanently stored on our servers

### 3.2 Camera Data
- **Purpose:** To scan medicine barcodes
- **Collected Data:** Camera images
- **Processing:** Barcode scanning is performed entirely on your device
- **Storage:** Camera images are not stored, only used for instant processing

### 3.3 Medicine Search Data
- **Purpose:** To fetch medicine information from the API
- **Collected Data:** The barcode numbers of the medicines you search for
- **Usage:** Used only to retrieve relevant medicine information
- **Storage:** Search history may be temporarily stored on your device

### 3.4 Advertising Data
- **Provider:** Google AdMob
- **Purpose:** To show you relevant ads
- **Collected Data:** Device identifiers, ad preferences
- **Control:** You can disable ad personalization from Google's privacy settings

### 3.5 Purchase Data
- **Purpose:** To manage In-App Purchase transactions
- **Collected Data:** Purchase status (ad removal)
- **Storage:** Purchase status is stored on your device and on Apple's servers

## 4. Purposes of Data Use

### 4.1 Core Services
- Displaying on-duty pharmacy locations
- Providing medicine information
- Offering barcode scanning feature

### 4.2 App Improvement
- Debugging and performance analysis
- Improving user experience

### 4.3 Advertising
- Showing ads to provide free service
- Managing ad removal purchases
